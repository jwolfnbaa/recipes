## Ingredients

- 4 oz bourbon
- 1 or sweet vermouth
- 4 dashes of bitters
- ice cubes
- 1 maraschino cherry for garnish


## Directions

Stir the bourbon, vermouth and bitters over ice. Pour into chilled martini glass. Garnish with cherry.
